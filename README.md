# Privacy Policy for FinSight

Last updated: March 19, 2026

## Overview

FinSight is a privacy-first personal finance app. **We do not collect, 
store, or transmit any of your personal or financial data.**
All data stays on your device at all times.

## Data Storage

All information you enter — transactions, categories, budgets, 
savings goals, and spending history — is stored exclusively on 
your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never 
leaves your device.

## Artificial Intelligence

FinSight uses Apple's CoreML framework for on-device AI features:
- Automatic categorization of merchants from receipt scans
- Weekly spending predictions based on your history

All AI processing happens entirely on your device. No data is 
sent to external servers for AI processing.

## Device Permissions

FinSight requests the following permissions:

- **Camera**: To scan physical receipts. Images are processed 
  on-device only and never uploaded anywhere.
- **Photo Library**: To import Apple Pay or bank screenshots. 
  Images are processed on-device only and never uploaded anywhere.
- **Notifications**: To send weekly spending summaries and 
  reminders. These are generated entirely on-device.

## No Accounts Required

FinSight does not require you to create an account. We have no 
access to your identity or financial information.

## No Third Party Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. There are no 
analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or external services 
integrated into FinSight.

## Children's Privacy

FinSight does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
